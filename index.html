Day 3: On the way on old stone age paths

June 22

The morning begins hectically. During the night, a rockfall has damaged the water pipe to the Memminger Hut and cut off the water supply. Washing? Not a chance. Mood and weather? Icy. We set off for Zams. The path leads us over the Seescharte, a rock gate that was already used as a pass in the Stone Age. A few hours later we stroll leisurely through Zams and treat ourselves to an ice cream before taking the cable car to the hut.
